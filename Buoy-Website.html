Kristen Lopez, Co-Founder

Kristen has built her career at the intersection of finance, investment strategy, and innovative business models. Her expertise spans financial modeling, asset management, and strategic planning within high-growth startups and emerging industries.

Most recently, she served as Senior Director of Asset Management at AutoCamp, a pioneer in the outdoor hospitality space. There, she led key initiatives across investment strategy and financial operations, helping shape the company’s growth trajectory. Kristen brings a deep passion for excellence, adaptability, and long-term value creation.

She holds a degree in Political Science from UCLA, graduating magna cum laude, and is currently pursuing her MBA at UCLA Anderson. She specializes in building robust financial models, optimizing asset performance, and driving data-informed decisions that support sustainable growth in dynamic startup environments.

When she’s not working on a project, Kristen can be found playing pickleball, volunteering locally, enjoying the outdoors, trying new restaurants, or traveling to explore new places.

Chiara DiMarco, Co-Founder

Chiara DiMarco is a former collegiate athlete with a deep-rooted passion for sports, equity, and community impact. She holds a degree in Business and Marketing from Cal State LA and brings nearly a decade of experience as a Research Administrator at UC Santa Barbara, where she managed multimillion-dollar grants focused on social sciences and equity research.

Her background blends operational expertise, strategic thinking, and a strong commitment to accessible, mission-driven work. Chiara supports small businesses and startups by helping them build solid foundations across marketing, client strategy, and administrative systems — always with an eye toward long-term impact.

When she’s not working, you’ll likely find her outdoors, coaching, watching sports, or traveling to new places.
